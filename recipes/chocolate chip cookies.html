Chocolate Chip Cookies

The best chocolate chip cookies are soft, chewy, and loaded with chocolate, achieved through careful ingredient ratios, proper butter handling, and optional chilling for texture.

Ingredients:

  • 200 g butter
  • 2 cups sugar (brown and white)
  • 2 eggs
  • 1-2 tsp vanilla essence
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 3 cups flour
  • 2 cups chocolate chips
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C)
  2. Combine flour, baking powder, and salt in a bowl and set aside.
  3. Beat the butter and sugars until creamy, then add eggs and vanilla.
  4. Mix the dry ingredients into the wet mixture until combined, then stir in chocolate chips.
  5. Drop by tablespoon onto ungreased cookie sheets and bake for about 8-10 minutes or until golden brown.
